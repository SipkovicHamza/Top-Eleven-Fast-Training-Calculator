How to use
  1. 1 Select the position of your player (ST, AMC, GK etc.) on the pitch.
  2. 2 Enter the required skills of your player in the fields below.
  3. 3 Press "Calculate Lowest Average" — this tells you which drill to use.
  4. 4 Choose a new skill or position for your player for testing fast training level. You can pause it after checking.
  5. 5 After training you'll get +1 or +2 progress. Enter results and repeat 7 total times, then calculate your Fast Trainer Type.
